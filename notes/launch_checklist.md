# Wayli first public launch checklist

A practical checklist for launching an early, credible version — not a mythical “perfect” one.

---

## 1. Core product ready (must-have)

### Student loan tool works reliably

- Calculator loads
- Results generate consistently
- No obvious crashes
- Inputs behave sensibly

### Logic feels trustworthy

- Salary changes affect outcomes
- Balance changes affect outcomes
- Plans differ logically

### Mobile usable

- Buttons tappable
- Text readable
- No broken layout
- Inputs usable on phone

---

## 2. User clarity (must-have)

### Explain what the tool does

Homepage should answer in 5 seconds:

- What is Wayli?
- Who is it for?
- Why should I care?

### Clear result wording

Users should understand:

- What their result means
- Why they got it
- What they might do next

### Plain-English disclaimers

- Educational estimates only
- Not personal financial advice
- Real repayments vary

---

## 3. Basic site structure (must-have)

### Navigation

Simple top nav is enough:

- Home
- Student loan tool
- Guides
- Contact

### Contact route

Even just:

- Feedback email
- Simple contact form

### About / trust section

Who built this and why.

People trust humans more than anonymous tools.

---

## 4. Content assets (strongly recommended)

### At least 2 live pages

Launch with:

- Why two people earning £45k can have different student loan repayments
- Should I overpay my student loan?

### Internal links to tool

Every page should naturally point users to calculator.

---

## 5. Technical basics (must-have)

### Domain + hosting live

- Custom domain
- SSL / HTTPS
- Fast load speed

### Error-free console (mostly)

Minor warnings are okay. Major crashes are not.

### Analytics installed

At minimum:

- Page visits
- Tool usage
- Button clicks

---

## 6. Launch messaging (must-have)

### Honest positioning

Use wording like:

- Free UK money decision tools
- Starting with student loans
- More tools coming soon

### Avoid overclaiming

Do not claim:

- Best in UK
- Perfect advice
- Guaranteed savings

---

## 7. Nice-to-haves (not blockers)

- Extra calculators
- Perfect branding
- Lots of pages
- Complex dashboards
- Newsletter funnels

Not needed for launch.

---

## Honest score for Wayli now

### Already strong

- Tool functionality
- Logic testing
- Clear niche
- Content direction

### Needs attention

- Navigation
- Polish
- Mobile pass
- Launch messaging
- Hosting readiness

---

## Realistic launch threshold

If these are true:

- Tool works
- Site looks credible
- Users understand value

You can launch.

---

## Recommended launch type

### Soft launch first

- Friends
- Relevant communities
- LinkedIn
- Small search traffic
- Feedback loop

Then improve quickly.

---

## Founder reminder

You do not need permission to launch.

You need a useful product people can use.

You are close.
