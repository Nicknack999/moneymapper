Make outputs feel specific, practical, and human.

- Overpaying doesn’t look urgent right now

# 3. DAD MODE (SIGNATURE FEATURE)

## Keep standard result as default.

Then offer optional CTA:

### Need the practical version?

**[ Dad Mode ]**

## When clicked:

Return tailored human guidance.

### If overpaying not ideal

If you were my kid, I’d say don’t rush to throw extra cash at this. Keeping flexibility may help more right now.

### If overpaying looks good

If you were my kid, I’d say this looks one of the clearer cases for overpaying — assuming savings are sorted first.

### If borderline

If you were my kid, I’d say this one sits in the middle. A balanced approach could make sense.

## Dad Mode Principles

- warm  
- grounded  
- practical  
- lightly witty  
- never patronising  
- confidence without preaching

## Why It Matters

This creates emotional differentiation competitors won’t copy well.
